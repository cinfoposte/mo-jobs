Purpose of consultancy: The United Nations Population Fund (UNFPA) is the lead United Nations agency for ensuring the sexual and reproductive health rights and choices for all. The work of UNFPA is based on the premise that all human beings are entitled to equal rights and protections. UNFPA focuses on women and young people because these are groups whose ability to exercise their right to sexual and reproductive health is often compromised. UNFPA's aspiration is to contribute to achieving the following transformative and people-centred goals by 2030:

  • end maternal death, and
  • end violence and harmful practices against women and girls.

In 2025 UNFPA enlarge its focus on increasing awareness among the population, including women, adolescent girls, partners, and families, on maternal and reproductive health and rights, women's empowerment, health literacy, informed choice in contraception, and bodily autonomy through the development and dissemination of communication materials, publications, and broadcasts.

The purpose of the consultancy is to support the strategic visibility, advocacy, documentation, and digital engagement of UNFPA Uzbekistan programme interventions under the 2026 Results and Resources Plan. The consultant will ensure consistent, high-quality, rights-based, and gender-sensitive communication aligned with UNFPA strategic priorities and the national reforms.

Scope of work:(Description of services, activities, or outputs)

National consultant on communication and social norm change will contribute to the development and dissemination of communication materials, publications, and broadcasts; monitor media coverage to ensure strong visibility; and analyze public feedback to inform and adjust communication approaches through the following:

Support to implementation of Communication Action Plan
  • Contribute to periodic update of the Communication Action Plan aligned with the 2026 Results and Resource Plan.
  • Support Integration of gender-transformative, rights-based, and evidence-based messaging into all communication materials.
  • Provide inputs to communication materials and talking points for senior management when required.
  • Support implementation of CP Outputs
  • Contribute to the development and implementation of communication and visibility materials aligned with UNFPA mandate, programme interventions, and donor requirements, ensuring compliance with UNFPA branding and visibility standards.
  • Support documentation of programme implementation, including field visits, workshops, awareness campaigns, events, and key milestones, and develop human-interest stories, results briefs, and publication-ready communication products.
  • Develop, adapt, and disseminate communication and IEC materials across print, digital, web, and social media platforms, ensuring bilingual content (Uzbek and English) where required.
  • Draft, edit, and publish communication content, including press releases, media articles, website updates, and social media posts, and coordinate production of multimedia materials (audio, video, and written content).
  • Support communication planning and coverage of programme events, trainings, media briefings, public discussions, and awareness campaigns aimed at social and behavioural change.
  • Contribute to communication aspects of partnerships, advocacy initiatives, and collaboration with media, civil society organizations, and other stakeholders.
  • Monitor media coverage and digital engagement, compile analytics and documentation, and provide recommendations to strengthen communication performance and outreach.
  • Support to Knowledge Management and Documentation
  • Collect, systematize, and archive photos, videos, testimonials, and communication outputs.
  • Support conducting professional photo sessions on programme thematic areas
  • Support maintenance of a structured repository of UNFPA programme -related communication materials.
  • Collaborate with designers, videographers, and photographers to ensure high-quality outputs.
  • Assist in preparation of procurement requests for communication materials and provide technical input into specifications.
  • Draft or support drafting Terms of Reference (TORs) for communication vendors.
  • Draft short impact briefs based on results and information collected from programme and aligned with indicators and milestones.
  • Contribute to quarterly newsletters (English and Uzbek).
  • Support tracking communication-related indicators (e.g., number of media partnerships, campaigns conducted)
  • Contribute to programme/ project donor with evidence of visibility and outreach results and support consolidation of programme documentation.
  • Support implementation of EU WEE social norm change activities
  • Support in organizing capacity building for journalists, content creators, and influencers on gender-sensitive reporting and inclusive SRHR communication.
  • Contribute to development and support in implementation of innovative and inclusive communication solutions to promote SRHR, gender equality, and transform harmful social norms
  • Contribute to interactive youth sessions in education institutions on gender equality, reproductive rights, and healthy relationships to promote informed decision-making on marriage and family life
  • Contribute to preparation for capacity-building workshops for religious leaders on gender equality, reproductive health, and prevention of gender-based violence implemented by IP
